About Me

Around the internet, my handle is typically "Dudemanguy". I like metal/rock music, programming/tech, and the stereotypical otaku crap (anime/manga/VN/etc).

In the past, I've had some crappy blogspots, but now I want to migrate to something with more direct control and freedom. Primarily, my postings here will be music-related (specifically album reviews), but I may post something else if I so desire.

I'm relatively active in a few different communities, so I won't list them all but here's a few. On the music side of things, I moderate Japanese Metal Forum (specialized forum for Japanese rock/metal) and Revelation of Doom (a general forum for metal music especially extreme metal). I also contribute to Forces of Steel which is another general metal forum.

For software, you can, of course, look at my github profile to see what projects I contribute to. I also maintain s6 packages for Artix Linux, a systemd-free fork of Arch that supports openrc, runit, and s6 as init systems.
